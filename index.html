About Us

Best Souvlaki
in your City

At Sufllaqeri Kupa, we bring authentic Greek flavors to your table with our traditional souvlaki recipes passed down through generations. Our master grill chefs carefully select the finest meats and marinate them in our secret blend of Mediterranean herbs and spices. Every souvlaki is grilled to perfection over an open flame, ensuring that signature smoky flavor and tender texture. We pride ourselves on using only fresh ingredients - from our homemade tzatziki sauce to our warm, fluffy pita bread baked daily. Whether you're craving classic pork souvlaki or our famous mixed meat platter, each bite transports you straight to the streets of Athens.
